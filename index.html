⚖️ Regulatory Compliance
1
ASRAA is designed to operate under Egypt's equity crowdfunding framework (Capital Market Law No. 95/1992 & FRA Decree No. 177/2024).
2
All deals are private placements restricted to accredited investors (net worth ≥ EGP 5M or annual income ≥ EGP 1M).
3
AML/KYC fulfilled under FRA Decree No. 161/2024 and Law No. 80/2002. All investors verified before access.
4
Acquiring ≥10% of a company's capital requires prior FRA approval. ASRAA flags this threshold automatically.
5
Startup investments are illiquid and high-risk. You may lose your entire investment.

The Process
01
Apply & Get Vetted
Submit your application. Our investment committee conducts due diligence and agrees a term sheet within 5 business days.
02
Deal Goes Live
Your round is listed and visible to 3,200+ accredited investors matched to your sector and stage.
03
Capital Raised into Escrow
Investors commit capital. All funds held by a third-party escrow agent — never by ASRAA — until the round closes.
04
Round Closes
Escrow releases funds to you. One SPV shareholder on your cap table — not hundreds of individuals.
05
Ongoing
Quarterly reporting applies. ASRAA manages investor relations and enforces SPV contractual rights on your behalf.
