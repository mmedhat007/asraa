⚖️ FRA Regulatory Compliance
1
Asra operates under Capital Market Law No. 95 of 1992 and is preparing for full FRA equity-crowdfunding licensing under Decree No. 177/2024.
2
All deals are private placements restricted to accredited investors (net worth ≥ EGP 5M or annual income ≥ EGP 1M). Public solicitation is prohibited.
3
AML/KYC obligations are fulfilled under FRA Decree No. 161/2024 and Law No. 80/2002. All investors are verified before access.
4
Acquiring ≥10% of a company's capital requires prior FRA approval under FRA Decree No. 178/2024. Asra flags this threshold automatically.
5
Startup investments are illiquid, high-risk, and may result in total loss. Past performance is not indicative of future results.
