ASRAA is a regulated distribution platform for professionally managed VC and PE funds. Before you proceed, we need to confirm a few things.
What is ASRAA?
ASRAA gives qualified Egyptian investors access to VC and PE funds that are normally only available to large institutions. Each fund on the platform has been through ASRAA's GP due diligence process.

This is not a stock trading app. Investments made through ASRAA are illiquid, typically held for 7–10 years, and carry significant risk of partial or total loss of capital.

ASRAA operates under Egyptian Capital Market Law No. 95/1992 and FRA Decree No. 177/2024.

1. Platform Nature

ASRAA is a fund distribution marketplace. It is not a fund manager, investment advisor, or broker-dealer. ASRAA does not manage investor capital or make investment decisions on behalf of investors.

2. Qualified Investors Only

Access to funds on ASRAA is restricted to qualified investors as defined under Egyptian Capital Market Law No. 95/1992 — individuals with a net worth of at least EGP 5,000,000 or an annual income of at least EGP 1,000,000.

3. AML / KYC

All investors are subject to identity verification and anti-money laundering checks under FRA Decree No. 161/2024 and Law No. 80/2002 before being permitted to commit to any fund.

4. Illiquidity Risk

Investments made through ASRAA are illiquid. You will not be able to redeem or sell your position during the fund's life, which is typically 7–10 years. There is no secondary market for these positions.

5. Capital at Risk

Past performance of a fund manager does not guarantee future results. You may lose part or all of your committed capital. Do not commit funds you cannot afford to lose.

6. No FRA Approval of Individual Funds

Funds listed on ASRAA are private placements and have not been individually approved or recommended by the Financial Regulatory Authority (FRA). ASRAA conducts its own due diligence but this does not constitute regulatory endorsement.

7. Conflicts of Interest

ASRAA earns a placement fee from fund managers for distribution services. This fee is disclosed in each fund's term sheet. ASRAA's economic interest may differ from yours as an investor.

8. Governing Law

All transactions on ASRAA are governed by Egyptian law. Disputes are subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of Cairo courts.
